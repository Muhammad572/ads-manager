Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report a problem?

Send us an email with your app name, device model, operating system version, and a description of the issue. Screenshots are always helpful.


How long does support take?

Most support requests receive a response within 24–48 business hours.


How do I request a feature?

We welcome suggestions. Simply email us your idea, and we'll consider it for future updates.

Before Contacting Support

  • Update your application to the latest version.
  • Restart your device.
  • Check your internet connection (if required).
  • Try reopening the application.
  • Include screenshots if possible.
